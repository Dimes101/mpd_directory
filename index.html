About the Department

Serving and protecting the communities of South Florida

Mission Statement

The mission of the Municipal Police Department is anchored in the unwavering commitment to serving the state of Florida as a whole, embodying an esteemed and highly proficient force that transcends mere law enforcement and extends into the realms of community stewardship and public safety. Dedicated to the relentless pursuit of excellence, the Police Department upholds an unequivocal determination to achieve and maintain an elevated standard of policing, exemplifying unwavering dedication, unimpeachable integrity, and exemplary professionalism in every facet of their operation.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in every action, maintaining the trust placed in us by the communities we serve.

Respect

We treat every individual with dignity and fairness, fostering positive community relationships built on mutual trust.

Accountability

We accept responsibility for our actions and decisions, maintaining transparency in all departmental operations.

Join the MPD

Begin your career with the Municipal Police Department

Become an Officer

We are always seeking dedicated individuals to join our ranks

Submit Application

Complete the application form. All applications are reviewed by command staff.

Academy Training

Complete the academy program. FDLE-certified external members may be exempt.

Field Training

Complete the FTO program under a certified Field Training Officer.

Probationary Period

Begin as a Probationary Officer. Demonstrate skills, SOP knowledge, and values.

Requirements

Activity Requirements

Probationary–Officer III: 3 hrs/week

Corporal: 4 hrs/week

Sergeant+: 5 hrs/week

Reserve: 1 hr/week

Promotion Criteria

Minimum 30 days at current rank. Must meet attendance, have no major DA in 30 days, demonstrate professional conduct, and pass required interviews for Corporal+ ranks.

Apply Now

External & Reserve Members

External Member Program

External members must complete the FTO program. Those with valid FDLE certification may skip the academy. Vehicle and uniform structures strictly enforced. Not permitted to join subdivisions. Reserve activity: 1 hour weekly.
